My love,

I have been trying to find the right words for you,
and I keep coming back to this simple truth —
you are the best thing that has ever happened to me.
Since 2007, you have walked beside me through everything.
The easy days and the difficult ones, the laughter
and the tears, the ordinary Tuesdays and the moments
that changed us forever. Through all of it, you were there.
You have given me three daughters who carry your kindness,
your grace, and your strength — and in them,
I see you every single day.
I want you to know that I do not take any of it for granted.
Not your patience, not your sacrifice,
and not the quiet, unwavering way you love our family.
On your birthday, my wish is simply this —
that you feel as loved and treasured today
as you have made me feel every day for nearly twenty years.

Forever yours,
your husband
